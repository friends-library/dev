June 25

I wish you to be serious and weighty in your attention to the concerns of the immortal part. It is no new thing for those who sincerely desire to serve and love the Lord to be tried with wanderings of mind, and the intrusion of unprofitable thoughts, even when sitting in religious meetings. But look steadily to the Lord, and cry unto Him, and in his own time He will send deliverance. Remember, to bear the cross is the way to win the crown. It is worthwhile to suffer many trials here, if they may but be sanctified to us, and work out for us, when the trials of time are over, an exceeding and eternal weight of glory.

—Thomas Kite
